About

A book, a method, and a decade of search work behind both

Cory Maki is the author of Reddit, AI Overviews & GEO: The SaaS Founder's Playbook for Winning AI Search Visibility, released April 2026, and the creator of the ARC Method — a 90-day framework that gives SaaS founders a sequenced way to earn citations in AI-generated answers. The book is the long version; the GEO pillar piece is the short version of the same argument.

Behind the book sits more than a decade of SEO and online reputation management work — startups, law firms, high-net-worth individuals, and a tour as Staff Editor and Business Development Manager at Grit Daily covering the SaaS ecosystem. Maki also founded and runs Digital Forge, a digital marketing operation he leads from Taichung.

His current focus is the discipline behind the book: why Reddit drives so much of AI citation behavior, why most "GEO tools" are solving the wrong problem, and how SaaS founders can build durable visibility in a search layer that rewards a different kind of authority signal.
